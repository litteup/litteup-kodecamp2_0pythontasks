Who are we and what do we do ?

Litteup Groups Inc. is a Nigerian conglomerate company based in Maitama in the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja. We are into IT Technology providing various IT solutions such as data optimization, Cyber security, Properties protection, Web development, Programming amongst others. We are also into AgroEconomics and we designs, setup and establish farms for starters and expansion purposes. Our farm product includes eggs for Chicken, Turkeys, Quail, Ostrich, Duck, Goose, Pigeon and Guinea fowl. Others are meats from the above mentioned birds and other animals such as Pork, Rabbit, Grasscutter, Goat, Ram and Guinea pigs. Additionally, we provide courses for all levels in both ICT and AgroEconomics. For Details of these courses, please visit our courses page.

We are an established group of company with over 10 years of experience and over 5000 staff across 30 states across the nation.

Services and Products Delivery

We partner with various logistics company to timely deliver our customers request across the nation at no cost to them.
